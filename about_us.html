Company Profile

Company was established in the year 1992 under the name BESI DRUGS PVT.LTD. to manufacture Bulk Drugs, Drug Intermediates, Specialty chemicals & Rare to find Chemicals. Our manufacturing facility is located in an industrial belt, Lakhamapur, Tal. Dindori , 200 kms from Mumbai and 30 kms from the Holy city, Nashik.
