Company Profile

        Company was established in the year 1992 under the name BESI DRUGS PVT.LTD. to manufacture Bulk Drugs, Drug Intermediates, Specialty chemicals & Rare to find Chemicals.Our manufacturing facility is located in an industrial belt, Lakhamapur, Tal. Dindori, 200 kms from Mumbai and 30 kms from the Holy city, Nashik. These chemicals are known for accurate composition, purity, highly reactive, high performance, effective and longer shelf life.

        We are backed by a team of highly efficient and competent work force, who are responsible to carry out our business operation in a dexterous manner. The chemicals offered by us are formulated using high quality ingredients and find their usage in various applications like disinfectant in hospitals, bleaching agent, oxidant & sterilizer, discoloring agent, deodorant, water treatment and food additive. Also, all these chemicals are processed at our in-house manufacturing unit.
