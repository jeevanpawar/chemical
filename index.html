Few words about us...

Company was established in the year 1992 under the name BESI DRUGS PVT.LTD. to manufacture Bulk Drugs, Drug Intermediates, Specialty chemicals & Rare to find Chemicals and became a pioneer in manufacturing and supplying of Chemical Products in a short span of time.

This products have enormous demand all across the nation and widely known for quality, efficiency and durability. Our manufacturing facility is located in an industrial belt, Lakhamapur, Tal. Dindori, 200 kms from Mumbai and 30 kms from the Holy city, Nashik. Our area of plot is about 30,000 sq mtr. (Private industrial land, free hold). Total buildup area is about 4800.00 Sq. Feet. Our products confirm to the international quality standards and offer excellent performance.
